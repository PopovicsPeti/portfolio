Currently, I'm studying German and Python on the Udemy education website. But, of course, I'm not letting my English and IT skills fade away. Although I don't have a university degree, I'm open to this kind of education too.

I really want to get into the IT field. The sector's expectations about self-improvement align with my goals for the future.
